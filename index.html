I was deeply inspired by the generative documentary Eno by Gary Hustwit. In the film there are several clips on Brian Eno's Oblique Strategies, which are a set of cards designed to break creative blocks and encourage lateral thinking. These sparked my curiosity so much that I decided to make my own, perhaps slightly less inspiring, version. If you're feeling stuck today, I hope this helps.
